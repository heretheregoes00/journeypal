---
description: Stress-test a decision or piece of work using research + critique
---

Take what's in $ARGUMENTS and run this sequence:

1. If external context is needed, dispatch the `researcher` subagent.
2. Generate a first-pass recommendation.
3. Dispatch the `critic` subagent to review it.
4. Revise based on the critique.
5. Present final recommendation with: revised analysis, what the critic caught, anything you chose not to address and why.

Do not skip steps. Do not soften the critic's objections.
